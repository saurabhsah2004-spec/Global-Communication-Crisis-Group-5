🌍 Global Communication Crisis

📜 Simulation Rules

Objective:
You are Global Managers handling international communication crises.
Your goal is to maintain trust and demonstrate cultural communication intelligence.

How It Works:
• The game lasts 8 communication rounds.
• Each round presents a real-world cross-cultural communication scenario.
• A country and cultural dimension will be involved.
• You must choose the best communication response.

Scoring:
✔ Correct Cultural Communication = +5 Trust & +3 Intelligence
➖ Partially Correct = +2 Trust & +1 Intelligence
❌ Wrong Communication = -4 Trust

Winning Condition:
After 8 rounds, the player with the highest Trust Score wins.

This simulation tests tone, framing, hierarchy sensitivity, negotiation style, and cultural awareness.
